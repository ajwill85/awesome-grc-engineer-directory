### About the role

- As a Security Compliance & Governance Team Lead, you will be responsible for leading the design, implementation, and continuous improvement of compliance and governance programs across the organization to ensure regulatory adherence, ethical conduct.

### Responsibilities

- Lead the design, implementation, and continuous improvement of compliance and governance programs across the organization to ensure regulatory adherence, ethical conduct, and sound corporate governance.
- Develop and maintain company-wide compliance strategies aligned with business goals, regulatory requirements, and risk appetite.
- Monitor and assess compliance risks related to laws, regulations, and internal policies; define and execute mitigation plans to minimize exposure.
- Drive and oversee third-party compliance due diligence, policy enforcement, and audit readiness activities.
- Establish and maintain frameworks for policy management, control monitoring, and issue remediation to ensure compliance maturity and operational integrity.
- Partner with risk management, legal, and internal audit teams to align governance initiatives with enterprise risk management practices.
- Use metrics, OKRs, and performance indicators to measure and continuously enhance the maturity of compliance and governance programs.
- Represent Compliance & Governance in cross-functional leadership forums and contribute to the strategic direction of the broader corporate governance function.

### Expected Qualifications

- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Information Security, or a related field (or equivalent experience).
- 7+ years of experience in compliance, governance, or risk management, with at least 2 years in a leadership or team management role. (http://role.Proven)
- Proven (http://role.Proven) experience in developing and implementing compliance management frameworks and governance policies. (http://policies.Strong)
- Strong (http://policies.Strong) understanding of regulatory environments such as PCI DSS, GDPR, ISO 27001 SOX, ETK or similar
- Demonstrated ability to collaborate across legal, risk, and business functions to drive compliance initiatives
- Analytical and critical thinking skills with the ability to leverage data for informed decision-making and prioritization
- Excellent communication and stakeholder management skills, including the ability to influence senior leaders
- Professional certifications such as CISM, CRMA, ISO 27001 Lead Implementer, or equivalent are preferredFluent in English (both written and verbal)
